About Us

DePoo:

Are you tired of stepping on dog poop? Want to enjoy your yard without the fear of the "landmines"? We can help! We provide reliable, affordable, convenient service. Our Job is to leave a poo free lawn! We will sweep the grassy areas while removing all dog poop and taking it with us. We will also use an enzyme spray that is safe for the lawn to remove that poop odor and bacteria, leaving you a clean and healthy lawn!

Owner:

Fred grew up in Colorado. That's right! One of a few natives left in Colorado he has grown up in the dog business with his father being part owner of a boarding kennel and grooming business. So, loving dogs is second nature. Fred is married to his beautiful wife of 26 years, has four awesome daughters, and 8 fantastic grandchildren. Fred always strives for perfection and a job well done. He will do the same for you!
